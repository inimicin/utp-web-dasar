Competition Rules

General Rules

Participants are high school students or equivalent
The competition is individual
Competition will be held in two stages, Online Qualifier and Final Day
Participants who are late are not given compensation for lateness
Participants who violate any rules will be disqualified

Qualifier Rules

Participants worked on the questions through the online judging website provided by the committee
Participants allowed to use C++ or Java
Participants were given 10 problem solving questions that had to be solved using allowed programming languages
Total working time 180 minutes
The 20 participants with the highest leaderboard scores will proceed to the Final Day stage which will be held offline

Final Day Rules

Final Day will be held in two days
On the first day, participants were given a real case study question
Participants are given a maximum of 4 hours to complete the given case study
Participants are required to submit a case study completion report in the form of a scientific article no later than 30 minutes after the maximum working time is up
On the second day, participants are required to present a case study completion report in front of the judges
The final leaderboard is the total result of the report scores and presentation scores of each judges
