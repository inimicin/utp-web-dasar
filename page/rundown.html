Notable Date

07 April 2023 | Registration Open

Registration open from 07 April until 07 June. If you want to take part in this competition, you can open this link to register yourself

09 June 2023 | Technical Meeting

Technical Meeting will be held online through Zoom Meeting. Everything that hasn't been regulated will be discussed here

12 June 2023 | Online Qualifier

First Qualifier will be held online by using Online Code Judging platform. Top 20 participant will advance to Final Day at University of Lampung this weekend

17 June 2023 | Final Day One

First Day of Final Qualifier. Participant will have to solve live case given at place. Final Score will be added to final leaderboard to determine top 3 participants

18 June 2023 | Final Day Two and Awarding Night

Second Day of Final Qualifier. Participant will have to present their solution for the case given at the Final Day One in front of judges. Score from each judges will be added to final leaderboard. Final leaderboard (with the score from judges) will pe revealed at the closing ceremony which will be held at 08.00 PM the same day
